Branding

Company Overview

What We Do

Hebrews Coffee offers premium, spiritually-inspired coffee blends crafted to elevate your daily ritual. Each blend is named after biblical figures and designed to reflect their virtues. We source the finest beans and roast them with intention, creating an experience that goes beyond taste.

Who We Are

We believe that every fiber of our experiences should be reflective, contemplative and purposeful. Coffee is weaved into the very existence of many people, and we want to offer more than just a beverage. Hebrews Coffee is the bridge between the act of drinking coffee and spiritual perception. Our brand is built on the principles of faith, quality, and intentional living.

Why Choose Hebrews

Customers are drawn to Hebrews because of the exclusive supply of pure Arabica beans in every product, ethically sourced and diverse in flavor profiles. Beyond quality, our brand resonates with those seeking a deeper connection in their daily rituals, infusing faith in the daily act of sipping coffee.

Brand Fonts

Display Font: BP Diet

Hebrews Coffee

Usage: Large headlines, hero text, and primary branding moments. Use sparingly for big impact.

Heading Font: Roboto Mono Bold

Pure. Purposeful. Premium.

Usage: Section headings, product names, and subheadings. Maintains a modern, structured feel.

Body Font: Roboto Mono Regular

Our coffee is cultivated from the gift of growth, roasted with intention, and brewed to awaken your senses. It is not mere coffee, but an experience filled with perception for the divine.

Usage: All body copy, descriptions, navigation, and UI text. Ensures readability and consistency.

Imagery & Art Direction

Photography Inspiration

  • Warm, natural tones: Soft lighting, earthy backgrounds, wooden textures

Design Elements

  • Minimalist layouts: Clean white space, structured grids, uncluttered compositions.
  • Biblical references: Incorporation of names, quotes, or symbols tied to each product.
  • Organic shapes: Rounded corners, flowing lines that mimic natural forms.
  • Typography as art: Bold, mono-spaced text treated as a visual element, not just communication.

Mood & Tone

Reflective, grounded, intentional. Imagery should communicate gently, leaving space for reflection and deep contemplation. Peace, stability and harmony are essential to the composition and color choices. Avoid contemporary, jarring imagery, loud colors, or overly busy designs.
